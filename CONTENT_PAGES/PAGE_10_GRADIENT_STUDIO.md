# Gradient Studio

Design and export custom gradients for your design system.

---

## Overview

The Gradient Studio is an interactive tool for creating and managing custom gradients for your design system. Use it to:

- Design custom gradients visually
- Export gradients in multiple formats
- Manage gradient tokens for your team
- Ensure color harmony and accessibility

---

## Features

- **Visual gradient builder**: Drag and click to create gradients
- **Multiple formats**: Export as CSS, JSON, or design tokens
- **Accessibility checking**: Ensure sufficient contrast
- **Team collaboration**: Share gradient libraries with your team
- **Integration**: Easily add gradients to your token system
